About

Everyone loves food. The world cuisine always attracts the food lovers. So what say, if we have an app that allows to have your food delivered at your doorstep from selected world cuisine restaurants of your city.

As a food delivery app, we maintained the uniqueness, fresh look and feel of the application.

This application is up and running in several countries like Singapore, Malaysia and India.

Our Solution

Easy to recogonise interface, top notch user experience with restraunt AI grouping,proper segeration of food categiores, and different types of other categories available like ready meat, groceries including frest fruits and vegetables.

Solution that we provided

The feature includes speed check, statistics about your performance,chat,mailbox and many more. This dosen't end here,we are core part of Factory Fresh client team to enhance and provide best user experience continuously.

Open Cart Feature

This feature allows you to see the cart and add more items from the main page.While your cart is open you can still shop for your favourite food and watch your cart simultaniously. Also make any changes in the cart, on the fly.

Process

To start with the project, we followed definite process including information gathering,user research,business analysis and competitive analysis.After we started creating information Architecture,data flows,user flows and basic wireframing. Thats how we gave the shape to the project into live application.
